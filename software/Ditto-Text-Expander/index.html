Ditto is the perfect tool to improve your typing productivity. It works with any program on your PC to substitute shortcuts you type with full phrases. For example, type ";ph" and Ditto will automatically type your phone number. Type ";adr" and Ditto will type out your full address.

No memorization required: you can also add text blocks to auto-complete phrases as you type. Ditto is easy to learn and easy to use. You'll be more productive instantly with a utility that grows with your skills... seamlessly.

Some common uses for Ditto include transcription, programming, work-related software, instant messaging, and any repetitive typing tasks that are time consuming and error prone.
